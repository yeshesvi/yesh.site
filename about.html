About Me!

Technologist and a gear head, that is what I am. From the pixel of a screen, the spinning sound of a hard disk to the roar of a V8 engine, I love everything. I am an avid rider, driver and computer builder. At the age of 16, frustrated with my expensive slow computer I started scouring the internet for information on how to build a proper computer. A year later, I built my own computer on a budget that was five times more powerful than my fathers expensive computer. From then on I was hooked, I have been learning more and more about technology and even majored in computer science.

As I mentioned before, I also love riding. I think riding a motorcycle is the closest most people can get to experiencing true freedom. The wind in your hair, no windshield, you and the machine together can go around the entire world. I think one of the primary reasons I love a motorcycle over a car is because a motorcycle needs you. It needs you to sit on it, it needs you to use all of your limbs and operate it, it needs you to balance it and it needs you to be pay 100% attention. One fine day a car can drive itself, but a motorcycle always needs its rider. A motorcycle is the most needy girlfriend / boyfriend you'll ever have.
